Encik Mohd Amirul Faliq bin Mohd Faizal

Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) (Hons.), UiTM

Encik Mohd Amirul Faliq bin Mohd Faizal read law at Universiti Teknologi Mara Malaysia, Shah Alam, wherein he graduated with Bachelor of Legal Studies (Hons.) with Second Class Upper in 2013. He also attained Second Class Upper when graduating with Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) (Hons.) from the same alma mater in 2014.

BACKGROUND

In June 2014, Encik Faliq chambered at Messrs. Othman Hashim & Co. under the guidance and supervision of Mr. Leslie Bala. He was exposed to general litigation matters as well as insurance litigation which includes fire claims, construction, professional indemnity and legal research. There, Encik Faliq received training to handle accident matters as well. He was called to the Bar in April 2015.

PRACTICE AREA

Encik Faliq joined Messrs. Athi Seelan Mahathir & Partners as a Legal Assistant in June 2015 wherein he was entrusted to handle Bodily Injury and Third Party Property Damage matters. He was given exposure to attend to various general litigation matters and building rapport with the firm’s clientele.

He joined this firm on the 4th of January 2016 as a Legal Assistant practising in the area of insurance matters where he is currently handling Bodily Injury and Third Party Property Damage claims.
