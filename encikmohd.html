Encik Mohd Taufek Bin Md Sani

LL.B (Hons.)(Malaya)

Encik Mohd Taufek Bin Md Sani graduated from University of Malaya with LL.B (Hons.) in 1993. He read in the chambers of Mr. Jargit Singh of Messrs Yusof, Jargit & Co. and was called to the Bar in April 1994. He joined Messrs Yusof, Jargit & Co. as a legal assistant practicing in the area of civil litigation. Thereafter, he joined Messrs Mustapha Ling & Co. where he served in the capacity of a legal assistant practicing in the area of Insurance and banking litigation. Subsequently in 2001, he joined Messrs Ngan Arifah & Chai as one of its senior legal assistants specialising in conveyancing and banking matters.

BACKGROUND

During his tenure for 11 years with Messrs Ngan Arifah & Chai, Encik Taufek handled all legal work involving land owners and Developers especially on acquisition of land for development. Besides that he was also involved in preparing under construction Sale and Purchase Agreement for house buyer as well as for sub-sales case. He was also actually involved in handling loan documentation for Banks. Amongst the Banks that he actively involved with are Citi Bank Berhad, Hong Leong Bank Berhad, Alliance Bank Berhad, Standard Chartered Bank Malaysia Berhad, CIMB Bank and RHB Bank with the total file handled between 300-500 files a year.

PRACTICE AREA

On the corporate side, Encik Taufek was also involved in drafting Joint Venture Agreement, Share Holders Agreement, Sale of Shares, Franchise Agreement, Distributorship Agreement and Agency Agreement. He joined with Messrs Murali B. Pillai And Associates in Feb 2013 as a partner and will be practicing in the area of corporate, conveyancing and banking practice and wills and trust and Syariah banking and Estate administrative law.
