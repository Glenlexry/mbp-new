Partners

Mr. Murali B. Pillai

LL.B. (Hons.) (Lon) Barrister-At-Law

Mr. Murali graduated with a Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of London with Honours and in 1982. He was called to the English Bar. In 1983, Mr. Murali was called to the Malaysian Bar and commenced practice as a legal assistant in the Firm of Messrs. James Foong & Anad where he was subsequently made a partner.

BACKGROUND

The partnership of Messrs. James Foong & Anad was dissolved when one of the partners, Justice Dato’ James Foong was appointed as a Judge of the High Court of Malaya. Mr. Murali then commenced practice as a sole proprietorship as Messrs. Murali B Pillai And Associates in 1990.


Mr. Murali has since being called to the Bar appeared not only in the High Courts but also in the Supreme Court and is experienced in all manner of litigation work.

PRACTICE AREA

Besides Litigation, Mr. Murali has also worked on a portfolio of corporate, conveyancing and loan documentation matters. Among these are Housing Loan documentation, Corporate Loan documentation, Licence Agreements, Joint Venture Agreements, Share Holders Agreement, Sale of Shares, Construction Contract, design supply and commissioning of Quarry Plants, Oil Palm Mills, Technical Assistance Agreement, Supply Agreements, Consultancy Agreements, IT Agreements and many others.


The legal work for the 1st Privatisation of a JKR quarry with the Selangor State Government as a shareholderi.e. Sungei Long Quarry was undertaken by Mr. Murali.


In the computer industry area, Mr. Murali has advised and drafted documents for both the Suppliers/Vendors of hardware and software and Purchasers of the same and also litigated on these types of contracts.
