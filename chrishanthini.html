Ms. Chrishanthini Sebastiampillai

LL.B (Hons.) Anglia Ruskin, CLP

Ms. Chrishanthini Sebastiampillai graduated with a Bachelor of Laws from Anglia Ruskin University in 1994. She obtained the Certificate of Legal Practice in 1996 and thereafter read in the chambers of Mr. Chandran G. Nair of Messrs Thevin Chandran & Wong. She then commenced her legal career as a legal assistant at Messrs Aziz & Mazlan where her area of practice was banking litigation, debt recovery, bankruptcy and general litigation.

BACKGROUND

In 1999, she joined the firm of Messrs G.K. Ganesan Saiful & Rokiah as a legal assistant, mainly in the area of civil litigation. Her portfolio included debt recovery, claims in contract, corporate litigation, actions arising from the torts of nuisance, defamation, and negligence and miscellaneous criminal matters. She thereafter joined Messrs Allen & Gledhill where her area of practice was general civil litigation. Her portfolio included debt recovery, claims in contract and tort, corporate litigation and labour matters. Ms. Chrishanthini joined our civil litigation department in August 2003. Her areas of specialisation include contractual disputes, debt recovery, defamation, labour law, personal injury, general insurance litigation, subrogation claims, negligence claims (mainly defence).

PRACTICE AREA

She has 17 years of litigation experience and has conducted matters in the Subordinate Courts, High Courts and Appellate Courts. She also has given talks and participated in legal clinics on the areas of constitutional rights and freedom of religion in Malaysia, inheritance, probate and intestacy. Ms. Chrishanthini was made a partner of the Firm on 1st January 2014.
