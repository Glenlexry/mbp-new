Puan Norhayati Binti Zainudin

LL.B (Hons.) IIUM LL.B (Shari’ah) (Hons) IIUM

Puan Norhayati read law at the International Islamic University Malaysia and graduated with Bachelor of Laws (Hons) with Second Class Upper in April 2008 and Bachelor of Law (Shari’ah) (Hons) also with Second Class Upper in April 2009. She is also fluent in Arabic Language (“Fusahah”).

PRACTICE AREA

Puan Norhayati joined this firm on 4th of March 2010 as a legal assistant and is currently practising in the area of Motor Claims Insurance and Takaful Litigation. She also advises on matters relating to Islamic family law, Islamic estate administration, Islamic law of succession and inheritance and wealth management as well as Islamic financial services.
